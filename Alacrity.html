About Alacrity

Hi, on behalf of Surendra Institute of Engineering & Management, Siliguri we hereby take immense pleasure to welcome you to the 6th edition of our annual cultural festival, ALACRITY VI. Alacrity, which comes from the Latin word alacritas. Though quite younger to most established festivals in the country, Alacrity receives immense participation from North East region. Alacrity provides the perfect platform to budding artists to show their mettle and talent. Alacrity brings to you two days of exaltation and promises to have you forget all your worries through its exhilarating rock shows, liberating classical performances, modish fashion shows, brain-storming quizzes and lots more. Besides this you can also participate in the techinal events organized by the respective branches of our college and much more such events are a part of Alacrity VI. We welcome you, whole heartedly, to our festival, where we shred all our furtive shackles, celebrate the juvenile, intrepid spirit in each one of us and raise a toast to the leitmotifs of Psychedelic life. Without you, this celebration of Alacrity would be incomplete.

BRIDGE IT


" CIVIL ENGINEERS are born to build and every structure in this world is the gift of civil engineering, from ancient time to this 21st century. One after another, skills with knowledge comes out with every new generation and we use it for our benefits, so this is the time to come up with your skills along with the knowledge of yours to make something innovative or somewhat modified structure of the existing ones. In this competitive world, competition is required and we have to compete. "



RULES & REGULATIONS


1. Maximum of four members are allowed in each group.

2. Bridge should be at least 650mm and maximum 1000mm long (clear span) and 200mm high.

3. Bridge must be no wider than 150mm. The weight of the bridge should not be more than 500 grams.

4. The width of each support at each end is 20mm(horizontal support) with balsa wood / popsicle sticks (6mm x 12mm x1000mm).

5. It should take minimum weight of 1 kg.

6. Materials to be used are:- i. ) Balsa wood sticks/ Popsicle sticks (6mm x 12mm x1000mm) thick and you may take length as per requirement. ii. ) 1 tube of quick drying epoxy glue. iii. ) 1 piece of 5m long string to tie (if required).

7. No extra string will be provided apart from mentioned length of string.

8. The bridge will be loaded at the centre, so the wooden pieces must have enough space both vertically and horizontally so that the load hanger can be hanged well.

9. While testing the bridge the load will be increased and it is tested to its extreme strength.

10. Vertical support will be provided by us to hold the bridge at both ends.
